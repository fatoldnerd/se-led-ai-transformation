SE-Led AI Transformation

From Task Force to Center of Excellence

A strategic proposal to position Sales Engineering as the driver of enterprise AI adoption

Executive Summary

Our CEO has positioned the company as AI-first. This is our opportunity to lead that transformation.

Sales Engineering sits at a unique intersection of technical credibility, GTM storytelling, and revenue visibility, making us uniquely qualified to drive AI adoption that's both technically sound and commercially viable.

This proposal outlines a three-phase approach to transform SE from AI consumers into AI orchestrators who drive strategic value across the enterprise.

The Opportunity

AI adoption is happening ad-hoc across our organization with no clear ownership or strategic coordination. Meanwhile, we're seeing a pattern at leading companies: SE teams are becoming the strategic drivers of AI transformation.

Why Sales Engineering?

Technical Credibility

Product trusts our evaluation and tool recommendations

GTM Storytelling

We translate technical capability into business value

Customer Insights

We ground AI strategy in real market needs

Revenue Visibility

We identify highest-ROI automation opportunities

No other team has all four capabilities. This positions us to bridge Product, Sales, Marketing, and Executive Leadership in ways no single function can.

Three-Phase Transformation

We'll execute this transformation through a three-phase model that builds momentum systematically:

1
Months 1-3

AI Task Force

Form a volunteer-based innovation team (4 SEs + 1 lead) to build quick wins and demonstrate value.

  • Hand-raisers only (22% participation target)
  • Ship 3 tools/workflows in 90 days
  • Executive showcase with ROI metrics
2
Months 4-9

Center of Excellence

Establish enterprise-wide AI governance with CTO partnership and SE as the anchor spoke.

  • Standards & best practices
  • Cross-functional enablement
  • Strategic communication to C-suite
3
Ongoing

Roadshow Program

Drive organic adoption through show-don't-tell demos to each department.

  • 15-minute impact demos
  • Real metrics from actual use
  • Peer-led learning and adoption

Self-Reinforcing Flywheel

Task Force → COE → Roadshows → Measurable Impact → Expansion
